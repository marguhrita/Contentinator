Privacy Policy

Contentinator is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information you provide to us when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

When you use our app, we may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information:
When you sign in to our app, we may collect personal information provided by you through the third-party authentication process. This may include your name, email address, profile picture, and other relevant information required for authentication.

User Content:
Our app may allow you to upload content (such as photos or videos) to your personal social media accounts. This content may be accessed and processed by our app solely for the purpose of facilitating the upload process.

Usage Data:
We may collect information about how you interact with our app, including the features you use, the content you view, and other actions you take while using the app. This information helps us improve the user experience and optimize our services.
